About IncomeAction.org

We don't waste your time with useless one-click petitions. Internet petitions have grown exponentially in number as advocacy organizations turned to online activism in recent decades. The influence and persuasiveness of ordinary internet petitions has declined in inverse proportion. We leverage our numbers to engage with decision makers and influence mass media in ways that can not be ignored.

View details »

An Effective, Focused Approach

If you can join in one telephone call to your congressional staff every two weeks, recording the outcome in our web forms, and join in writing an email to mass media or a regulatory agency every other week, then you will soon realize how the influence of our collective action exceeds the sum of its individual efforts. The legislation and regulations we call and write about change, but the underlying issue—income equality—remains the same.

View details »

Targeted and Persistent Actions

Our actions are carefully selected topical, incisive questions on specific legislation, regulation, issues, and upcoming committee hearing subjects. Our calls are followed by faxes to the staffer we reached reminding them of our questions and their commitment to answer them. Responses are recorded in a public database. If we don't hear back in two weeks, we prompt our volunteers to follow up with another phone call and fax.
